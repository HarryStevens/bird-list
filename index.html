6,239 birds have been struck by planes at JFK, LaGuardia and Newark airports between Apr. 13, 1990 and Oct. 30, 2013. Some birds have been less lucky than others. Here are the ten bird species that have been struck the most.

Herring gulls have had it the worst, but other birds have been hit a lot too. It's pretty hard being a bird around lots of giant airplanes. It can be very dangerous.
